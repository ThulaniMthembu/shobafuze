History

Shobafuze Interline Express is a reputable bus service company with over 20 years of experience, operating since 2001 in the transportation industry. Established with a commitment to providing reliable, safe, and comfortable travel, Shobafuze Interline Express has become a trusted name for passengers seeking efficient and enjoyable journeys. With a focus on customer satisfaction, the company continually strives to enhance its services and maintain high standards of operation.

Shobafuze Interline Express began its operations by transporting scholar children in the Ekurhuleni area. Over the years, the company has expanded its services and now operates in the Midvaal scholar district, providing transportation across local routes within South Africa. This growth reflects the company's dedication to meeting the evolving needs of its customers while maintaining its core values of safety, reliability, and comfort.
